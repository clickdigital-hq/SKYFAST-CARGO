How It Works

Our Shipping Process

Five simple steps from China factory to your doorstep in Tanzania.

🏭 1

Send to China Warehouse

Ship your goods from your supplier directly to our warehouse in China.

🔍 2

Inspection & Packaging

We inspect, weigh, document, and professionally package your cargo.

✈️ 3

Air Transportation

Your cargo flies via trusted airline partners to Julius Nyerere Airport.

🛃 4

Customs Clearance

Our team handles all TRA customs procedures and import documentation.

🏠 5

Delivered in Tanzania

We deliver to your door anywhere in Dar es Salaam and major cities.
